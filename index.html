Problem Statement

Visitors to the Colorado Wildlife website often struggle to find the information they need due to difficult navigation and outdated content. Mobile users find the site particularly challenging, and the lack of interactive features makes learning about wildlife conservation less engaging. Additionally, the website is not fully accessible to everyone, including those with visual impairments. To better connect with our community and support our mission, we need to redesign the website to be more user-friendly, up-to-date, engaging, and accessible for all visitors.

User Testing
